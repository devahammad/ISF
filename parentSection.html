Parental Interface

To meet the Principal:

The Principal is normally available to parents from 8.30 am to 1.00 pm and from 2.00 pm to 4.00 pm, on all working days or by prior appointment.

To meet the Vice-Principal:

The Vice-Principals are normally available to parents from 10.00 am to 01.00 pm, on all working days or by prior appointment.

To meet the Teacher:

Parents may meet the teachers by prior appointment and with the permission of the vice principal concerned only on Thursdays from 1.00pm to 1.45 pm

MORE ABOUT THE SCHOOL

ISF has the best experience reflecting in its capable heads of departments (H.O.D.s). Our HODs come from varied experiential background with proven and meritorious years in the education field, thus offering you the best-in-class methodology and expertise. Our HODs are dedicated to our motto of being "Committed to Change". They are part of a continuous evolving process to keep improving their teaching methods, in imparting the best knowledge to our students.we have also committed ourself to the vision of raising aspirations,enriching partnerships,and striving for achievements.it is through this that we create a focus on students success. our school has build a reputation for the quality of it's work from developing teaching and learning to the extracurricular opportunities.
